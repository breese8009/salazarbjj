Kids Classes

We have kids classes for ages 5-10 years old. Jiu Jitsu is not only great for discipline, but also is a great way to teach kids how to properly defend themselves.

Adult Classes

If you have been doing Jiu Jitsu for just 6 months and you get into an altercation with someone who has not trained at all, YOU have the upper hand and will most likely get the best of the situation. Adult Jiu Jitsu is great for self defense, fitness and builds confidence.

Cascao Affiliate

Lalo is an affiliate of Walter Cascao in Las Vegas Nevada. Anyone that trains with Lalo can also train at all of Walter Cascaos affiliate schools as well through out Las Vegas.
